About Me

I'm an 18-year-old from the Philippines and the 1st child in the Lada family. While my family are filled with photographers, I've forged my unique path in video editing to diversify our interests. Currently, I'm a full-time student at Algonquin College, enrolled in the Interactive Media Design Program, eager to expand my knowledge for a promising future.

I'm also passionate about assembling collectibles like Lego and 3D puzzles. Solving them, with or without instructions, is a rewarding challenge. When I'm not collecting, I'm a competitive gamer, specializing in fast-paced first-person shooters. I've even participated in Valorant Esports competitions, where I earned both money and great memories.
